We recognise the Traditional Owners of Country throughout Australia, their diversity, histories and knowledge plus their continuing connection to lands, waters, sky and community. While we're located on unceeded Whadjuk Noongar land, we pay our respects to all Australian Indigenous peoples and their cultures; to their Elders past and present

We pay our respects to those amongst the lesbian, gay, bisexual, trans and intersex communities who have worked to support the improved health and wellbeing of their peers, children, families, friends and country.

Our definition of women includes all women. Likewise, our definition of men includes all men: cisgender, transgender or gender divers. We also acknowledge and include those who identify beyond the binary. We honour the Elders in the diverse communities of which we are a part of and we celebrate the extraordinary diversity of people's bodies, genders, sexualities and relationships they represent.
